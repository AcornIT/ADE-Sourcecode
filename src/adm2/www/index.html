ADM2

The Application Development Methodology (ADM) is a component-based development paradigm that enables you to develop and share business logic across a variety of applications. SmartObjects allow you to define and reuse business logic components, offering code inheritance, encapsulation, and other object-oriented development benefits.

SmartObjects, such as SmartDataFields, SmartToolbars, SmartDataViewers, SmartDataObjects and SmartBusinessObjects, provide you with the ability to quickly create, customize, and assemble components. There are also specially purposed SmartObjects that facilitate B2B messaging over the Internet via the Progress SonicMQ Adapter.
